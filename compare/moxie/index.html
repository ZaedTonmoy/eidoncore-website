Moxie is built for freelancers. Eidoncore is built for agencies that scale. See where each platform excels and which fits your growth trajectory.

Quick Verdict

The short version

Moxie is a polished freelancer tool with proposals, contracts, e-signatures, time tracking, and accounting. It's ideal for solo operators who need a single business hub.

Eidoncore is designed for agencies with teams. It adds real-time messaging, a service catalog with checkout, advanced reporting, and better team scaling — all with a free tier Moxie doesn't offer.
