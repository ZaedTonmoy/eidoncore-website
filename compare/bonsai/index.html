Eidoncore vs Bonsai

Bonsai is a freelancer-first accounting and business tool. Eidoncore is an agency operating system. See which platform matches your ambitions.

Quick Verdict

The short version

Bonsai shines for solo freelancers who need proposals, contracts, accounting, and tax prep in one place. It's laser-focused on the financial side of freelancing.

Eidoncore is designed for agencies with teams and multiple clients. It offers deeper project management, real-time messaging, a service catalog, automations, and comprehensive reporting — areas where Bonsai falls short.

Decision Guide

Who should choose which?

Choose Bonsai if you…

  • Are a solo freelancer needing accounting
  • Need proposals, contracts, and e-signatures
  • Want tax prep and expense tracking
  • Need time tracking tied to invoices
  • Don't need team messaging or Kanban boards

Choose Eidoncore if you…

  • Run an agency with a growing team
  • Need Kanban boards & deep project management
  • Want built-in real-time messaging
  • Need a service catalog with online checkout
