Catalog & Checkout

Clients browse, select, and pay — all in the portal

Your service catalog lives inside the client portal. Clients browse your offerings, add services to their cart, fill out intake forms with project requirements, and check out securely through Stripe — no email back-and-forth required.

  • Branded service catalog within the portal
  • Shopping cart with multi-service support
  • Intake forms capture project requirements
  • Stripe-powered secure payments

Digital Assets

Clients manage their own products

When clients purchase digital products, a dedicated Digital Assets page appears in their portal sidebar. They can download files, view delivery instructions, submit intake forms, and cancel recurring subscriptions — all self-serve.

  • Product download with delivery URL
  • Delivery instructions per product
  • Intake form submission from product page
  • Self-serve subscription cancellation with reason
  • Purchase timeline and renewal info
Guest Checkout

Sell to visitors — no signup required

Your service catalog is public. Visitors can browse, purchase through Stripe, and an account + project is automatically created after payment. Zero friction checkout.

  • Public catalog visible to anonymous visitors
  • One-step checkout with registration + payment
  • Automatic account and project creation after payment
  • Client can log in immediately and see their project
  • Apple Pay & Google Pay supported
My Account

Client-side financial management

Clients get a My Account page to manage subscriptions, view payment history, and update billing info — reducing support requests and giving them full transparency.

  • Active, paused, and past subscriptions with status
  • Combined spending summary and payment history
  • Saved payment methods (card brand, last 4, expiry)
  • Self-serve subscription cancellation
  • Invoice payment and service purchase history
