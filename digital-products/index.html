How It Works

Create, publish, sell — in minutes

1. Upload your files

Upload any file type — PDFs, ZIPs, videos, images. Set a price, title, description, and preview image.

2. Publish to your catalog

Your digital product appears alongside your services in the public-facing service catalog. Clients can browse and add to cart.

3. Collect payment via Stripe

One-click checkout with Stripe. Buyers get instant access to download their purchase — no manual delivery needed.
