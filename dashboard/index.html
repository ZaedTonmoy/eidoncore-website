Project Health

Spot at-risk projects before they derail

Every active project is automatically scored based on task completion and deadline proximity. Green means on track. Yellow means at risk. Red means off track — and you need to act.

  • On Track: ≥50% tasks done, no overdue milestones
  • At Risk: <50% done, some overdue, or due within 7 days
  • Off Track: <25% done and past due, or >50% milestones overdue
  • Color-coded health indicators across all projects
Team Workload

See who's overloaded in seconds

The workload panel shows task distribution across your entire team — by status, by member, by urgency. Spot bottlenecks and rebalance work before deadlines slip.

  • Tasks by status: To Do, In Progress, In Review
  • Per-member task load with overload flagging (10+ active = flagged)
  • Top 5 overdue tasks sorted by urgency
  • Completion rate percentages per team member
  • Estimated vs. logged hours comparison
Smart Alerts

Know what needs attention — right now

Dashboard alerts surface critical issues the moment they happen. No digging through pages or checking spreadsheets — problems come to you.

  • Critical: Overdue invoices, off-track projects, failed automations
  • Warning: Stale tasks (3+ days), expiring contracts (30 days)
  • Info: Inactive clients, unassigned tasks, pending draft invoices
  • Click any alert to jump directly to the issue
  • Dismiss alerts — they reappear if the condition persists
