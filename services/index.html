Clients browse your catalog through the branded portal, add services to cart, fill out intake forms, and pay directly via Stripe. No back-and-forth — just seamless commerce.

  • Client-facing catalog browser

Tracking & Reviews

Track delivery, collect reviews

Once a client purchases a service, Eidoncore tracks hours logged, credits consumed, and milestones completed. Clients can leave star ratings and written reviews, building social proof for your catalog that future clients can see.

  • Hours and credits tracking per purchase
  • Milestone-based delivery tracking
  • Client star ratings and written reviews
  • Review moderation and publishing controls
  • Service performance analytics
