The Challenge

Dev agencies need more than a Jira board

Your developers live in sprint boards, but the business side — client management, invoicing, proposals, and reporting — is scattered across a dozen tools. You need a platform that bridges the gap between engineering work and business operations.

  • Billable hours lost because devs forget to track time
  • Client communication in email while project updates are in Slack
  • Sprint velocity insights disconnected from revenue data
  • Manual invoice creation from exported timesheet data
Sprint Management

Kanban and list views built for engineering teams

Run your sprints in Eidoncore with full-featured Kanban boards and list views. Create tasks with story points, assign developers, set sprint deadlines, and track completion. Drag tasks between columns as work progresses — Backlog → In Progress → Code Review → Done.

  • Kanban boards with custom columns matching your sprint workflow
  • List views with sorting by priority, assignee, or due date
  • Task dependencies and subtask management
  • Sprint-level budget tracking (hours or story points)
Time-to-Invoice Pipeline

Every billable hour becomes revenue automatically

Developers log hours directly on tasks. Eidoncore maps each developer's hourly rate to their logged time, calculates totals per project, and generates invoices with detailed line items — breaking down exactly what the client is paying for.

  • Per-developer billing rates (junior, mid, senior, lead)
  • Automatic invoice generation from tracked hours
  • Detailed time reports your clients can verify
  • Support for T&M, fixed-price, and retainer billing models
Client Visibility

Give clients a window into your sprint progress

Instead of writing weekly status emails, let clients see it themselves. The client portal shows project timelines, completed milestones, and outstanding work — giving your clients confidence without you spending hours on reporting.

  • Real-time project dashboard visible to clients
  • Milestone completion updates with automatic notifications
  • Scope change requests managed directly in the portal
  • Transparent billing — clients see exactly what they're paying for
