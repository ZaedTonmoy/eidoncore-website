The Challenge

Consulting is relationship-driven — your tools should be too

Consulting firms thrive on deep client relationships, but most project management tools treat every engagement the same. You need CRM capabilities alongside project tracking, time management alongside invoicing, and reporting alongside client communication.

  • Client relationships tracked in spreadsheets or legacy CRMs
  • Engagement scoping and billing done manually in documents
  • Consultants' utilization rates unknown until it's too late
  • Executive dashboards require exporting data from 3+ systems

Know your team's utilization rate in real time

See which consultants are at capacity, who has availability, and where billable hours are being spent. Eidoncore calculates utilization rates automatically and ties every tracked hour to the right engagement and the right invoice.
