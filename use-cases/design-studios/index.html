Design studios deserve better tools

Stop managing creative projects in spreadsheets and chat threads. Eidoncore gives your design studio a dedicated workspace for every project, client, and deliverable.

The Challenge

Creative work needs creative management

Design projects are inherently iterative. Feedback loops, revision rounds, and approval gates make traditional project management tools feel clunky. Your team needs a tool that understands how creative work flows — from brief to final delivery.

  • Revision feedback buried in email chains and Slack messages
  • No clear visibility into which deliverables are approved vs. pending
  • Time spent on revisions not properly tracked or billed
  • Client expectations misaligned because there's no shared workspace
Visual Workflow

Kanban boards built for creative teams

Move tasks through your creative pipeline — Brief → Wireframe → Design → Internal Review → Client Review → Approved. Drag cards between columns, attach design files, and tag team members directly on each task.

  • Custom columns matching your creative pipeline stages
  • File attachments and inline comments on tasks
  • Priority labels and due dates for delivery management
  • Workload view to prevent designer burnout
Client Portal

Share work and get approvals — no email needed

Invite clients to their own branded portal. They see the project timeline, review deliverables, leave feedback, and approve work — all in one place. No more "Can you resend that file?" emails.

  • White-label portal with your studio's branding
  • Clients can approve, request changes, or leave comments
  • Full revision history — no more lost feedback
  • Automatic notifications when deliverables are ready for review
Milestone Invoicing

Invoice at every milestone, not just at the end

Structure your projects with milestone-based billing: 30% on briefing, 40% on design approval, 30% on delivery. Eidoncore generates invoices automatically as milestones are completed and sends them to clients.

  • Milestone-based payment schedules
  • Automatic invoice generation on milestone completion
  • Professional branded invoices with your studio's identity
  • Payment tracking with automatic overdue reminders
