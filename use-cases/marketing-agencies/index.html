Campaign Management

Organize every campaign as a project

Create a project for each campaign or retainer. Set budgets (monthly retainer hours or fixed-price), assign team members, define milestones like "Creative Brief → Design → Review → Launch", and track progress in real-time.

  • Retainer-style projects with monthly hour budgets
  • Milestone-based workflows matching your campaign stages
  • Automatic health scoring — know when a campaign is going over budget
  • Team workload visibility across all active campaigns
Client Collaboration

Give clients their own branded portal

No more email chains for approvals. Your clients log into a white-label portal where they can view project progress, approve deliverables, download reports, and pay invoices — all under your agency's branding.

  • Branded client portal with your agency's logo and colors
  • Clients see real-time project status and task progress
  • File sharing and deliverable approvals in one place
  • Integrated invoice viewing and online payment
Reporting & Invoicing

From hours tracked to invoice sent — automatically

Your team logs hours against campaigns. Eidoncore calculates billable amounts based on each team member's rate, generates professional invoices, and sends them to clients with one click. Monthly retainer reports are generated automatically.

  • Time tracking tied directly to projects and tasks
  • Automatic invoice generation from billable hours
  • Revenue reports broken down by client, campaign, and team
  • Payment tracking with overdue reminders
