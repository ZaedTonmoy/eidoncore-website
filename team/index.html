Roles

5 built-in roles for every team shape

From founder to freelancer — each role has carefully scoped permissions that match real-world agency workflows.

  • Owner: Full access including billing, plans, and team management
  • Admin: Full operational access — settings, branding, projects, invoices
  • Project Manager: Manages projects, tasks, team assignments, clients
  • Accountant: Invoice creation, payments, billing, financial reports
  • Team Member: Assigned tasks, time logging, own work management
Custom Roles

Build your own permission matrix

Built-in roles not flexible enough? Create custom roles with toggle-based permissions across every feature area — projects, tasks, invoices, messaging, services, and more.

  • 30+ individual toggle permissions
  • Organized by: Projects, Finance, People, Services, Settings
  • Internal comments visibility control
  • Can't delete roles with active members
  • Change roles anytime — users are notified
Workload

Never let anyone burn out silently

The workload dashboard gives managers visibility into how tasks are distributed — with automatic capacity flagging when someone hits overload.

  • Per-member task counts by status (To Do, In Progress, In Review)
  • Automatic capacity status: Normal, High, or Overloaded (10+ tasks)
  • Completion rate percentages per member
  • Overdue task tracking per member
  • Estimated vs. logged hours comparison
  • Filter by project, status, and date range
Profiles

Click on any member to see their complete profile — identity, status, work metrics, billable hours, active projects, and recent activity.

  • Name, email, avatar, role, department, employment type
  • Status: Active, Invited, or Suspended
  • Work metrics: tasks assigned, completed, overdue, completion rate
  • Total billable hours across all projects
  • Active project assignments
  • Per-workspace display name and avatar support
